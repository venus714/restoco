What We Do

Restoco web app runs on a remote server and provides a unique opportunity for landlords to monitor the situation of their premises in real time. The systems are automated, and the client (tenant) has a reminder set to pay rent. For this, however, we charge the tenant Kshs 150 per month payable with the rent. The tenant shall incur this cost of Kshs 150. The landlord shall remit the amount collected to Restoco together with the management fee agreed upon.

Land and House Brokerage

For any land and houses sold, Restoco Limited shall charge 5% of the total value of the land. If Restoco is involved in negotiations, Restoco shall charge 6% inclusive of the negotiation fee payable by the seller. The fee shall be paid Kshs to Restoco ltd, 14 days after the payments are made.
